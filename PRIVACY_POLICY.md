## 1. Data We Collect

- **Location data** — your device's GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude) and city name, used solely to fetch weather for your location. This is never stored on our servers.
- **App preferences** — temperature unit, wind speed unit, and time format, stored only on your device.
- **Saved locations** — location names and coordinates you manually add, stored only on your device.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Location coordinates are sent to **Apple WeatherKit** and **Open-Meteo** to retrieve weather and climate data. No other identifying information is sent. Search queries (city names you type) are sent to **Apple MapKit** to resolve locations.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

| Service | Provider | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|---|
| WeatherKit | Apple Inc. | Current & forecast weather | apple.com/legal/privacy |
| Open-Meteo | Open-Meteo.com | Historical climate data | open-meteo.com/en/terms |
| MapKit | Apple Inc. | Location search | apple.com/legal/privacy |

---

## 4. Data Storage

- All preferences and saved locations are stored **locally on your device** using iOS standard storage.
- Weather data is cached locally and automatically expires (10 minutes to 2 hours).
- We do not operate any servers or databases.

---

## 5. What We Don't Do

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No crash reporting sent externally
- No advertising or third-party data brokers
- No sharing or selling of any data

---

## 6. Location Permission

The app requests **"While Using the App"** location permission only. Your location is never accessed in the background.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
